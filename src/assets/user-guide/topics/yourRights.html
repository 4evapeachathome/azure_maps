Legal Right / Your Rights

The Legal rights page have the content that explains about Know your rights, Domestic and Gender Based Violence, Emegency Rights and Safe leave.


1. The user inputs a valid URL to launch the Education Module and the URL is : Your Rights


The Admin user must access the CMS site in order to add, edit, and update the material on the page..

Go to CMS > Content Manager > Select and open the 'LegalRight' under the Single type from the side menu.

2. Below the title of the page , there are some content section which shows the description for each topics for educational purpose.


New Entry / Update the content and its images.
For add or updating the images and content , Go to CMS > Control Manager > Select and open 'LegalRight' > Click and open the related abuse and do the changes

Update the content if required and Click on Publish to make the changes refelects in the application.
