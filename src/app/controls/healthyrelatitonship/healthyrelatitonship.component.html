Healthy Relationship

Recognizing signs of HEALTHY and UNHEALTHY RELATIONSHIPS

Healthy Relationship In a healthy relationship, both partners share responsibilities evenly, promoting balance and mutual respect. This division of tasks—whether related to household chores, financial obligations, or other duties—ensures that neither partner feels burdened or taken for granted. Instead, both individuals feel valued and supported. Open communication Open Communication is essential for understanding each other's needs and desires in a healthy relationship. Partners who engage in active communication address concerns as they arise and express their thoughts clearly and directly. This honesty and openness strengthen their bond, making the relationship more resilient. These themes relate to important aspects of relationships, including trust, support, respect, commitment, and connection.
